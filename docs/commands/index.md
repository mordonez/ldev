## Namespace overview

Main entry points:

- `doctor`, `start`, `status`, `logs diagnose`
- `context`, `portal check`, `portal inventory`
- `resource`
- `db`
- `deploy`
- `ai`
- `worktree`
- `osgi`

Use `ldev <namespace> --help` for the full options of your installed version.
